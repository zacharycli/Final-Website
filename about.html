Our Team

We are a STEM team from Colchester Royal Grammar School made up of six Year 12 students. We are an experienced team, with highlights at the National and International Finals of the Lego League competition. This year, we are competing in multiple STEM competitons, including F1 in Schools and The Big Bang Fair.

All of us study STEM related subjects, with ambitions to go into Engineering, Architecture, and other STEM fields at University.
