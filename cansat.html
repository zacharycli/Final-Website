F1 in schools Big Bang Fair CanSat

We are pleased to announce that we have entered the ESA Canstat competition With a larger team. Our design centers around photogrammetry: taking photos of the surface of the earth and building them into a 3D model. To allow us sufficient time in our descent, we will have a parafoil, controlled in two axes by a pi zero. Currently, our research focuses on designing the can from the components we have, and trying to simulate the environment virtually to write an autopilot.
