F1 in schools Big Bang Fair CanSat

We are pleased to announce that we have entered the ESA Canstat competition With a larger team. The competition involves making a minature satellite within the dimensions of a can.

We have chosen too design our CanSat to carry out early exploration of an expolanet, however it coudld also have applications on earth. Our design centers around photogrammetry: taking photos of the surface of the earth and building them into a 3D model.To allow us sufficent time for this on our descent, we will be manufacturing a parafoil and are currently investigating using 2 axis control of the parafoil too further control our descent. We also plan too carry out the new and revolutionary aerogel particle capture, which uses a special material called silica aergel too obtain particles, which can then later be analysed.



Hugh's innovative design, after being tested using Computational Fluid Dynamics, defeated the designs submitted by the rest of the team.
